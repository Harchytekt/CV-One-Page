Personal Details

Who am I ?

Born June the 21st, 1994 in Mons, Belgium, I grew up surrounded by my five siblings along with my parents.
Despite my taste for reading and my attitude homebody, I practiced many activities including tennis, swimming, gymnastics, judo and even dancing.

It is around 15 years that I discovered the world of computers: Windows was not the only operating system, sites and programs were written by means of specialized languages.
Then I started using Ubuntu, and I created my first website and some basic programs in C, Python and Perl.
This experience made me realize what the world of computers and what it would be possible for me to accomplish with good knowledge.

After high school, having decided to turn to computer science, I choose to go to Science Faculty of the University of Mons in Computer Science section where we were encouraged to use Unix (mainly Linux). I learned there how computers work and the basics of programming through two high-level languages (Python and Java) and a low level (MIPS).
Physics, mathematics and projects allowed me to improve the designing of complex algorithms as well as my time management.

After two years, I redirected myself to network, completely unknown field for me. So I began a bachelor in Computer Science and Systems with Professional Focus in Networks and Telecommunications at the Haute École en Hainaut, where I am currently studying.
Besides learning network functioning in partnership with Cisco, I also reinforces my bases in the working of computers as well as in programming through learning the C and C# languages.
